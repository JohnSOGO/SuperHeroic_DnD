Final Rites of Saint Elira Virelle, Flamebearer of the Kindled Mercy

Blessed flame, guide this soul beyond the veil.
Burn away her sorrow, but not her name.
Elira Virelle, you who bore mercy in silence,
Let your heart be known again in the light.

By the fire of Ignis Solara, we return you to peace.
May no false oath bind you, no shadow claim you.
Rest now, Saint Elira, Flamebearer, Witness, and Martyr.
Your light shall kindle others still to come.
