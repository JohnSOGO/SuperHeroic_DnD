🛡️ Shield Master Cheat Card (for Chris)

⚔️ Shove with Your Shield

  • If you take the Attack action, you can use your Bonus Action to shove a creature within 5 ft.
  • Choose to either:
    • Knock the creature prone
    • Push it 5 feet away
  • This uses a Strength (Athletics) check vs their Strength (Athletics) or Dexterity (Acrobatics).
🌀 Special (1/Long Rest): Automatically succeed on one shove attempt using your restored shield.

🛡️ Dexterity Save Defense

  • Add your shield’s +2 AC bonus to Dexterity saving throws against effects that target only you (like Fireball or Breath Weapon).

🌀 Reaction Block

  • If a Dex save allows half damage, and you succeed, you can use your Reaction to take no damage.
  • This mimics the Rogue’s Evasion, but uses your shield!
🧪 Bonus Effect: If wielding the Shield of the Forgotten Vow, it may whisper or scream near undead or Elira’s Idol — revealing presence or warning danger.
