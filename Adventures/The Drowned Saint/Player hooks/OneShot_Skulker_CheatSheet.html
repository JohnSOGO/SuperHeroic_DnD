🎯 One-Shot's Stealth & Skulker Cheat Sheet

This guide helps One-Shot (your rogue assassin) make the most of stealth, surprise, and the Skulker feat — especially in dark, dangerous places like the sunken cathedral.

🕵️ What Skulker Lets You Do

  • Hide in Dim Light: You can hide even if there's just low light, mist, or shadow. You don’t need full darkness or total cover.
  • Miss Without Revealing: If you attack while hidden and miss, you stay hidden!
  • See in Dim Light: You make Perception checks in dim light without disadvantage. Undead can’t sneak up on you easily.

🗡️ Assassin Tips

  • Always Try to Start Combat Hidden – You get Assassinate: advantage AND auto-critical hits if the enemy hasn’t acted yet!
  • Use Steady Aim – If you don’t move on your turn, you get advantage. That gives Sneak Attack even without allies nearby.
  • Use Cunning Action – Every turn, you can Dash, Disengage, or Hide as a bonus action. That means you can vanish again right after attacking.

📌 Combat Strategy

  • Hide behind a broken pew or rubble in the cathedral.
  • On your first turn, use your bow for a sneak attack from hiding — it might crit!
  • Then bonus action: Hide again (Cunning Action) or reposition.
  • Let the others distract the enemies — you pick off key targets like ritual casters or Toiling Shades.
  • If it gets dangerous, you’re fast — Tabaxi speed can double your movement for an escape.

💡 Final Advice

Stay in the shadows. Choose the moment to strike. Let the others be seen — and when the moment comes, drop your enemy before they even blink.
