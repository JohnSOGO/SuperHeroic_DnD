🛡️ Chris Plot Hook: The Shield in the Mist

Inside the submerged cathedral lies a crumbling chamber — sealed behind a heavy stone door perforated with holes. A steady stream of pale-green mist leaks out, reeking of rot and sanctified decay.

Only a creature immune to poison and able to change shape could slip through alive. Only Chris can make the attempt.

🔐 Entry Conditions

  • The wall is solid stone with hand-sized bore holes.
  • The room beyond is filled with acidic divine mist (poison damage to non-resistant creatures).
  • Chris can ooze through safely due to his plasmoid nature and poison resistance.

🛡️ Shield of the Forgotten Vow

Inside, resting atop a broken altar, is a half-melted shield engraved with the sigil of Brother Seril — a warrior-priest who tried to stop Elira’s ritual drowning but was cast aside.

  • When touched by Chris, the shield partially reforms.
  • With Mending, the shield becomes usable.
  • Once per long rest, Chris may automatically succeed on a Shield Master shove or block.
  • The shield hums with divine tension. In the presence of the Idol or the bishop’s ghost, it begins to whisper Elira’s name — or scream.

💧 Ichor in the Air

The mist is not just poison — it’s sacred. Chris can condense a vial of Elira’s sanctified ichor from the air, usable in the final ritual:

  • Can be poured on the Idol to weaken its defenses (disadvantage on saving throws against sealing)
  • Can be used as a one-time radiant weapon, dealing 3d8 radiant damage to undead
  • But collecting it leaves a fragment of Elira’s sorrow inside Chris (DM's discretion)

This chamber and its contents are . His unique physiology and shield mastery allow him to retrieve tools and relics no one else can — and face a haunting echo of the past.
