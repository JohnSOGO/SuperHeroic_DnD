📘 The Hidden Assassin’s Codex

Deep within a collapsed confessional of the cathedral, One-Shot uncovers a small, leather-bound book — stitched together from veiled prayers and black market vellum. It is the Codex of the Hollow Veil, a hidden manual for divine assassins who once worked in secret for the clergy.

“To kill without sound, to wound the soul, to silence what should not rise.”

🧠 Only for One-Shot

The Codex only reveals its contents to a trained assassin. As soon as One-Shot touches it, glyphs glow faintly and rearrange into a readable format only she can see. Her rogue instincts feel an almost eerie familiarity, as if this knowledge was waiting for her.

📜 What It Reveals

  • Secret notes on how the clergy used assassins to eliminate heretics — including dissenters from Elira’s order.
  • Names scratched out, but one chapter is titled: “The Girl They Couldn’t Bury.”
  • Details on anti-undead pressure points and ritual bindings.

💀 Ritual Kill Technique: Sever the Unquiet

Once per long rest, One-Shot may invoke this technique when making a melee or ranged weapon attack against an undead creature:

  • Bonus Action: Whisper the Codex’s death phrase ("Vellum fades").
  • Effect: If the attack hits, the target must succeed on a DC 13 Constitution saving throw or be:
    • Staggered until the end of its next turn (disadvantage on all attacks and checks)
    • Unable to cast spells that require verbal components for 1 round
  • On a critical hit, the creature is also turned for 1 round as if by a Turn Undead effect.
“Undeath fears silence. Strike the word from their breath.” — Codex of the Hollow Veil

🎭 Why This Matters

This Codex gives One-Shot a way to interact meaningfully with the supernatural in this adventure. It ties her skills as an assassin directly into the lore of Elira’s downfall — and may grant her the unique ability to disrupt the Soul-Drinker Idol’s defenses, or protect the ritual team when it matters most.
