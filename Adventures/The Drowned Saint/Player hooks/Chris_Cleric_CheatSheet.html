✨ Chris the Cleric — How to Be Awesome!

You're Chris — a cool, oozy plasmoid who heals friends and blasts bad guys with holy magic. Here’s how you can be the hero every time:

🛡️ Your Best Powers

  • Bless – Make your friends better at fighting! Use it before a big battle.
  • Guidance – Help someone do something hard (like picking a lock or sneaking). Just touch them and say “you got this!”
  • Healing Word – Your teammate is down? Say a magic word and they get back up!
  • Light – Light up a dark place. You’re the team flashlight!
  • Spare the Dying – If someone is about to die, you can keep them safe. Big hero moment!

🤔 When Should I Use What?

  • Before Battle: Use Bless to help fighters and One-Shot hit their attacks!
  • When Someone Falls: Use Healing Word or Spare the Dying
  • In the Dark: Use Light on a rock, coin, or staff
  • When Someone is Doing Something Tricky: Use Guidance to help!

🌟 How You’re a Hero

  • You keep everyone safe 💖
  • You help the group stay strong 💪
  • You can stop monsters with holy power ✨
  • You are brave even if you’re gooey 😄

💬 Helpful Things to Say

  • “I cast Bless on you guys! Go get 'em!”
  • “Don’t worry, I got you—Healing Word!”
  • “Need help? I’ll use Guidance!”

Have fun, Chris. Your team needs you!
