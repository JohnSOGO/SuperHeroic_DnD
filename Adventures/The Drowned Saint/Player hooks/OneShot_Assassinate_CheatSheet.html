🎯 One-Shot's Sneaky Cheat Sheet

💜 You’re amazing, One-Shot!

You're fast, clever, and super dangerous when no one sees you coming. Here's how to make the most of your sneakiness with Assassinate and Steady Aim.

🗡️ Assassinate - When You Get the Drop!

  • Advantage on attacks against enemies who haven’t gone yet.
  • Critical hit if they are surprised and you hit!
  • Sneak Attack damage is doubled on critical hits.

Best time to use: When you're hiding and combat hasn't started yet, or you're ambushing!

🎯 Steady Aim - Stand Still, Strike True

  • Use your Bonus Action to gain Advantage on your attack.
  • You can’t move at all on your turn if you use this!
  • Works with both sword and bow.

Use Steady Aim: When no one else is near the enemy or you want to make sure your sneak attack hits.

Don't use it: If you're already hidden or have an ally next to the enemy (you’ll get Sneak Attack anyway!).

🧠 Sneaky Girl Genius Tips

  • Try to hide before combat starts — you're strongest when unseen.
  • Use your Tabaxi speed to find the perfect angle.
  • If you miss a shot from hiding, you stay hidden (thanks to Skulker)!
  • Advantage = Sneak Attack. Always look for ways to get it!

🌟 You’ve Got This!

You’re not just called One-Shot — you are the one shot that matters. Pick your moment, aim true, and make the bad guys wish they never saw you coming. Have fun and be awesome!
