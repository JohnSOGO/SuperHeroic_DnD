✨ The Ember Shrine of Elira

“A flame that never yields. A whisper that sanctifies steel.”

📍 Location

Hidden beneath a collapsed apothecary near the drowned cathedral is a fractured shrine — once tended by Elira’s order. A scorched altar rests atop a cracked marble plinth, beneath which a copper brazier glows faintly through layers of soot and ash.

🧠 Chris’s Cleric Knowledge

As a cleric and plasmoid, Chris instinctively recognizes this shrine as one of Elira’s last true sanctified sites. The ember within is not mundane fire — it is a fragment of divine flame, preserved through centuries of drowning rot. Chris recalls the ancient rite of Sanctum Ignis, used to bless relics and purge corruption.

🔧 The Blessing Rite (3 Steps)

The party may perform this rite to bless one weapon each. Chris automatically understands the proper flow of the ritual, reducing the difficulty and guiding others.

  1. Ignite the Ember – Chris removes the ash with reverence. He must speak a prayer or guiding verse to rekindle it.
    Skill: Religion (Chris, DC 11)
  2. Anoint the Blade – Each player dips their weapon in the flame after applying water or ichor.
    Skill: Sleight of Hand (One-Shot, DC 13) or Survival
  3. Speak the Vow – Each character recites a vow of purpose or penance aloud, invoking Elira’s mercy.
    Skill: Insight or Persuasion (DC 13)

✅ Results

  • Each blessed weapon counts as magical for 24 hours (or one long rest).
  • The first time it hits an undead, it deals an extra 1d4 radiant damage.
  • Optional: The weapon glows faintly near corrupted relics or during the sealing ritual.

❌ Failure

  • If the ritual is rushed or fumbled, the flame shrieks and a Drowned Clergy animates from the ash.
  • Defeating it allows the flame to stabilize and grants the blessing with reduced power (no radiant bonus).

🎭 Roleplay Highlights

  • Chris sees a vision of Elira kneeling in prayer, her voice hoarse but unbroken.
  • Aloquin feels one of his burdened souls whisper “Stay the blade…” before fading.
  • One-Shot hears a ghostly child’s voice: “Do it quick. Like always.”
DM Tip: Let Chris’s leadership during the ritual reduce one roll’s DC by 2 for each other player.
