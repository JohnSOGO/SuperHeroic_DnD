⛯ Inscription of Saint Elira Virelle

Here lies Saint Elira Virelle, Flamebearer of the Kindled Mercy,
Mercy’s Last Witness, Keeper of the Emberlight, and Shepherd of the Condemned.

She who wept for the wicked and burned for the innocent.
Whose flame did not falter, even when cast into the deep.

By her breath, the fire of Ignis Solara endured.
By her silence, a hundred sins were pardoned.

Let none forget the warmth she gave, nor the light she was denied.
And should her name pass your lips — do so with clean hands and a lit heart.
