🪦 Aloquin's Soul Credit Cheat Sheet

This guide helps determine when Aloquin Velshire earns credit toward his Deathwarden soul tally. He must release, cleanse, or guide a soul — not simply destroy it.

✅ When Aloquin Gets Credit

  • ☑️ He kills a soul-bearing undead directly with weapon, spell, or pact ability
  • ☑️ He uses Grasp of the Deathwarden, Last Rites, or Soul Shepherd on a valid target
  • ☑️ The enemy dies within his Well of Souls or Last Rites aura
  • ☑️ He speaks a Deathwarden prayer or symbolically releases the soul at the moment of death
  • ☑️ He wounds or marks the soul before another player delivers the killing blow

⚠️ Conditional Credit

  • 🔸 If another player kills the creature but Aloquin was spiritually involved (e.g. aura, wound, command), it may count
  • 🔸 If a Tolling Shade or twisted echo is purified (not destroyed), it can count as credit

❌ No Credit If...

  • ✖️ The soul is devoured or imprisoned (e.g. by the Soul-Drinker Idol)
  • ✖️ The kill occurs outside of Aloquin’s influence or with no spiritual intention
  • ✖️ The target is a construct or soulless creature (e.g. Sanctified Shell)
  • ✖️ The soul is shattered or obliterated with no chance for passage

📿 Rituals & Actions That Qualify

  • Last Rites (1/long rest): Aura that counts soul releases in combat
  • Obsidian Shard: Killing undead with this weapon always counts
  • Well of Souls: Spirit-binding zone — kills inside always count
  • Deathwarden Verse: If spoken while dealing final blow, adds symbolic weight
